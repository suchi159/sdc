Non-Disclosure Agreement

Confidentiality
You will have access to candidate PII (Personally Identifiable Information), exam content, and screen recordings. You agree to keep all such information strictly confidential and to use it solely for proctoring duties on the SecureProctor platform.

Non-Disclosure
You will not copy, distribute, publish, or disclose any exam materials, candidate data, or recordings to any third party. This obligation survives the termination of your certification.

Terms & Conditions

1. Integrity Policy
As a certified proctor, you are responsible for maintaining the integrity of all assigned examinations. Any collusion or failure to report violations will result in immediate revocation of your certification.

2. Certification Scope
Your SDC Proctor ID certifies you to proctor the specific certificate course selected during registration for in-class examinations.

3. Certification Renewal
Proctor certifications are valid for 1 year from the date of approval. You must renew prior to expiration.
